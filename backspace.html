About BACKSPACE 2K26 - Technical Events for Participants

BACKSPACE 2K26 is a National Level Technical Symposium organized by SBM College of Engineering and Technology, Dindigul. This prestigious event brings together talented team participants from across the country to showcase their skills in programming, multimedia, and technical innovation. Join our exciting technical events with flexible team size options and network with fellow tech enthusiasts.

6 Diverse Events

Compete in Paper Presentation, Code Debugging, Quiz, Multimedia, E-Sports, and Connection challenges

Recognition & Prizes

Winners will be recognized and awarded for their outstanding performances

Complete Provisions

Refreshment, Kit, and Lunch provided for all registered participants

Affordable Registration

Only Rs.150 per participant
Choose up to 2 events of your choice
(One Technical and One Non-Technical)

General Rules & Instructions

Rules for All Events

  • College ID Card: Mandatory for all participants
  • Event Limit: Each participant can participate in maximum 3 events
  • No Alterations: No changes allowed after finalizing events
  • Bring Your Own: For Paper Presentation, bring your PPT in a pendrive
  • Final Decision: Judges' decision in all rounds is final

What's Included

  • ✓ Refreshment & Kit for all registered participants
  • ✓ Lunch for all registered participants
  • ✓ Certificate of Participation
  • ✓ Prizes for Winners

Registration Fee

Rs. 150 per participant

Limited to 3 events per participant

Technical Events for Team Participants

Technical Events

1. Paper Presentation

Team Size: Maximum 2 participants per team
Format: Font: Times New Roman
Font Size: Main Heading 24pts, Headings 16pts, Heading2 14pts, Main Body 12pts
No page numbers, headers, or footers
Duration: 10 minutes presentation
Last Date for PPT Submission: 06/02/2026
Intimation for Paper Selection: 09/02/2026
Last Date for Registration: 11/02/2026
Requirements: Submit soft copy of presentation & full paper via email. File name should be your team name.

2. Code Debugging

Team Size: Single participant
Languages: Java, Python
Duration: 1 hour
Topics: Operating Systems & Data Structures
Structure: Two rounds

3. Quiz

Team Size: Single participant
Duration: 1 hour
Topics: Operating Systems & Data Structures
Structure: Two rounds

Non-Technical Events

4. Multimedia

Team Size: 2 participants
Duration: 30 minutes
Note: Bring your own requirements for this event

5. E-Sports

Team Size: Maximum 4 participants per team
Duration: 30 minutes
Note: Bring your own requirements for this event

6. Connection

Team Size: Maximum 4 participants per team
Duration: 20 seconds per question
Structure: Two rounds
Format: Connect pictures to form words
Rules: Each team must announce a leader at the time of event. Top 5 teams selected for Round 2.
